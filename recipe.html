Recipe for Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and spices. It is a staple food in many West African countries, including Nigeria, Ghana, Liberia, Sierra Leone, and Gambia. It is also popular in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom.

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 1kg
Onions 2
Tomatoes 3
Pepper 1
Garlic 2 cloves
Thyme 1 teaspoon
Curry 1 teaspoon
Vegetable Oil 1 tablespoon
Water 1 cup

Method

  1. Boil the water
  2. Boil the rice
  3. Spice the vegetables
  4. Boil the chicken
  5. Put the rice in the pot
  6. Put the chicken in the pot
  7. Put the vegetables in the pot
  8. Put the spices in the pot
  9. Put the oil in the pot
  10. Mix and leave to cook
